## What is PluralBuddy?

Plurality bots are mostly a modern concept on Discord in recent years. However, they are needed for numerous reasons, whether that be for reasons that make somebody more comfortable chatting (DID/OSDD), or for other purposes, plurality bots overall increase the positive atmosphere that a server has.

PluralBuddy is a plurality bot, made to raise the bar of how easy to use and setup a pluarality bot is. Plurality bots are complex in nature due to their structure and overall commands needed to complete something that needs to be done. PluralBuddy aims to solve this exact issue due to making things very straightforward to get done, along with different modes of configuration depending on what is needed.
